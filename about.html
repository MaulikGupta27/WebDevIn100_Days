About WebDevIn_100Days

Empowering developers through 100 days of hands-on web development projects

100+ Projects
10K+ Developers

Our Mission

Making web development accessible, practical, and fun

Learn by Doing

We believe the best way to learn web development is through hands-on practice. Our 100 projects are carefully crafted to provide progressive learning experiences.

Community Driven

Our platform thrives on community contributions. We foster an environment where developers of all levels can learn, share, and grow together.

Real-World Skills

Every project is designed to teach practical skills that you'll use in real-world development scenarios, from basic HTML to advanced JavaScript frameworks.

Our Vision

To create the world's most comprehensive and accessible web development learning platform. We envision a future where anyone, regardless of their background, can master web development through practical, project-based learning.

  • Bridge the gap between theory and practice
  • Make learning web development enjoyable and engaging
  • Build a supportive global developer community
  • Continuously evolve with industry trends

Our Story

The journey that started it all

The Idea

2023

It all started with a simple observation: traditional web development tutorials often lack practical application. We wanted to change that.

First Launch

Early 2024

We launched with our first 10 projects and quickly gained traction in the developer community. The response was overwhelming and encouraging.

Community Growth

Mid 2024

Our community grew to thousands of developers, and contributors from around the world started adding their own projects and improvements.

100 Projects Milestone

Late 2024

We reached our goal of 100 unique projects, each designed to teach different aspects of web development in an engaging way.

Today

2025

We continue to grow and evolve, adding new technologies, improving existing projects, and building the future of web development education.
