Tank Fighter

Instructions

Single Player Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys: Move and rotate tank

Spacebar: Fire primary bullets

Shift: Fire secondary weapon (missiles)

Two Player Controls

Player 1: WASD + G (shoot) + F (missiles)

Player 2: Arrow Keys + Enter (shoot) + RShift (missiles)

Objectives

• Destroy all enemy tanks to advance levels

• Collect power-ups to gain advantages

• Avoid enemy fire and obstacles

• Survive as long as possible

Power-ups

Health Pack - Restore HP

Shield - Temporary invincibility

Speed Boost - Faster movement

Double Damage - More power
